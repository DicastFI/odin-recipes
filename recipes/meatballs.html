Odins´s Meatballs

Description

Meatballs are easy and fun to make from scratch. This recipe is very simple and uses just a few ingredients to create classic homemade meatballs. They're perfect for all of your favorite recipes, including spaghetti and meatballs, sandwiches, soups, and game-day snacks.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbs water
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1/2 ts salt
  • 1/8 ts black pepper
  • 1 pund lean ground beef

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Heat the oven to 350 F.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the egg, water, breadcrumbs, onion, salt, and pepper and combine. Break the ground beef into chunks, then add it to the bowl. Mix gently, but thoroughly, with your hands to combine. Don't overmix.
  4. Form the mixture into meatballs about 1 inch in diameter and place on a broiler pan or a pan with sides topped with a wire rack.
  5. Bake at 350 F for 25 to 30 minutes until meatballs register 165 F on a meat thermometer.
