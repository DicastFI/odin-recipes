Odin´s Garlic Bread

Description

Fresh and flavorful Garlic Bread warm from the oven! Also known as the perfect beginning to any Italian style dinner. You get those perfectly crisp edges, a soft buttery center, and a golden brown toasted top with fresh garlic and herbs.

Ingredients

  • French bread
  • Butter
  • Garlic 3 gloves
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Cut french bread loaf in half horizontally.
  2. In a small mixing bowl stir together butter, garlic, parsley, basil, oregano and pepper.
  3. Spread garlic butter mixture evenly over both bread halves. Place bread on baking sheet if you haven’t already.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 8 minutes.
  5. Move oven rack closer to broiler. Let bread broil about 45 seconds to 3 minutes until golden brown. Keep an eye on it, it can brown quickly.
  6. Cut into slices and serve warm. Season with a pinch of salt if needed.
